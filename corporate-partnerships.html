Corporate partnerships

Corporate partnerships are available to corporations and organizations who want the option to fund development of specific functionality. As a part of a corporate partnership you will receive dedicated development time from the Defold Team and we will work with you to define which projects to work on.

In addition to this, corporate partners can get prominent mentions on the Defold site, in newsletters and in the Defold editor. Corporate partners will also get invitations to participate at special events hosted by the foundation.

We offer the following levels of corporate partnership:

Platinum

$48000/year ($4000/month)
  • Your company logo, name and link in the Platinum corporate partner section on the Defold site and GitHub
  • Your company logo and name on the Editor Splash Screen
  • Your advert in the bi-weekly Defold newsletter
  • Your company logo and name on marketing material
  • Your company name mentioned in presentations
  • You will be invited first to participate in special events
  • 640 hours of development time

Gold

$24000/year ($2000/month)
  • Your company logo, name and link in the Gold corporate partner section on the Defold site and GitHub
  • Your company logo on the Editor Splash Screen
  • Your advert in the bi-weekly Defold newsletter
  • Your company logo and name on marketing material
  • Your company name mentioned in presentations
  • You will be invited after Platinum partners to participate in special events
  • 320 hours of development time

Silver

$12000/year ($1000/month)
  • Your company logo, name and link in the Silver corporate partner section on the Defold site and GitHub
  • Your company logo and name on marketing material
  • Your company name mentioned in presentations
  • You will be invited after Gold partners to participate in special events
  • 160 hours of development time

Bronze

$6000/year ($500/month)
  • Your company logo, name and link in the Bronze corporate partner section on the Defold site and GitHub
  • Your company logo and name on marketing material
  • Your company name mentioned in presentations
  • You will be invited after Silver partners to participate in special events
  • 80 hours of development time

If you are unable to find a suitable corporate partnership level you can reach out to us at partner@defold.se to discuss a custom partnership level.
