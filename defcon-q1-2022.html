DefCon is an online event for the Defold community, hosted by the Defold Foundation. Get the latest updates from the Defold team, listen to interesting and inspirational presentations and participate in an open Q & A with the Defold team.

Agenda

All times in CET.

19:00
State of Defold

Björn Ritzl
During this presentation we'll share some of the highlights of Defold in 2021 and go into detail on the plans for Defold in 2022.

19:15
How we ended up using AI in a game about AI

Marius Petcu / Critique Gaming
In this presentation Marius will show how his team gave the AI in Good Morning AI a voice using Amazon Polly and a sound effect stack.

19:30
Intro to TS-DEFOLD

Justin Walsh
A Crash course into using TypeScript with Defold using the ts-defold project. We will cover setting up a project, demonstrating the tooling available, and answer that burning question of "why"?

19:45
Building a first person shooter in Defold

Artsiom Trubchyk / Indiesoft
In this presentation Artsiom will share some of his tips and tricks and his way of working when creating a first person shooter using Defold.

20:00
The making of Plague Lords

Konstantin Slepchenkov / Red Unit Studios
Plague Lords is a dark fantasy strategy game set in the mid 14th century during the Black Death. Learn how Konstantin and his team at Red Unit Studios used Defold to their advantage throughout the development process.

20:15
Blender Sync Tool

David Lannan
David will demonstrate how to use the Blender Sync Tool plugin to get almost instant import from Blender to an open Defold project.

20:30
Open Q & A with the Defold Team

Björn Ritzl, Mathias Westerdahl, Alexey Gulev
The evening will end with an open Q & A session with the Defold Team. The team will answer questions posted in the YouTube Live chat as well as questions asked in advance in the Defold forum. Ask your questions in advance here.



Join

The event is free and requires no registration. Simply join the YouTube Live stream of the event or watch the recording at your own convenience.
