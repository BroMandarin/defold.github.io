Donate and support Defold

Defold is funding development through community donations, corporate partners, partnership deals and support contracts. We accept donations of any size from individuals, organisations and corporations.

As a software foundation we rely on community donations and corporate partnerships to ensure the long term sustainability of the project. You can make monthly donations using Patreon or one time donations using PayPal. The donations and partnerships are used for:

  • Hiring developers to work on Defold
  • Pay for hosting of services
  • Purchase of hardware for testing and development
  • Pay for travel expenses when visiting conferences

Community donations

As a monthly contributor to the Defold Foundation you help ensure the continued development of Defold. As a contributor you will receive mention on the Defold website and newsletter and you will be able to use a badge on the Defold forum highlighting you as a contributor.

Corporate partnership

As a corporate partner you will get a direct line of communication to the Defold Foundation and the development team and a chance to directly influence the development of Defold. You will also be able to prominently show your company logo and name on the Defold website and in communication from the Defold Foundation.

We offer the following levels of corporate partnership:

Platinum

$48000/year ($4000/month)
  • Your company logo, name and link in the Platinum corporate partner section on the Defold site and GitHub
  • Your company logo and name on the Editor Splash Screen
  • Your advert in the bi-weekly Defold newsletter
  • Your company logo and name on marketing material
  • Your company name mentioned in presentations
  • You will be invited first to participate in special events
  • 16 man-weeks of development time

Gold

$24000/year ($2000/month)
  • Your company logo, name and link in the Gold corporate partner section on the Defold site and GitHub
  • Your company logo on the Editor Splash Screen
  • Your advert in the bi-weekly Defold newsletter
  • You will be invited after Platinum partners to participate in special events
  • 8 man-weeks of development time

Silver

$12000/year ($1000/month)
  • Your company logo, name and link in the Silver corporate partner section on the Defold site and GitHub
  • Your advert in the bi-weekly Defold newsletter
  • You will be invited after Gold partners to participate in special events
  • 4 man-weeks of development time

Bronze

$6000/year ($500/month)
  • Your company logo and link in the Bronze corporate partner section on the Defold site and GitHub
  • You will be invited after Silver partners to participate in special events
  • 2 man-weeks of development time
