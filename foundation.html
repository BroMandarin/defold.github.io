The Defold Foundation

The Defold Foundation owns and develops Defold.

The Defold Foundation was founded by King at the same time as Defold was made available as an open source project in December 2019. King donated all rights to Defold, including the Defold trademark and source code, to the foundation and tasked it with the continued development of Defold as an open source project.

The Defold Foundation is registered in Sweden under the name "Stiftelsen Defold Foundation". The foundation is governed by Swedish law and overseen by the County Administrative Board in Stockholm. The foundation board consists of representatives from King, the Defold team and the industry.

Objectives

The foundation objectives as submitted to the County Administrative Board in Stockholm:

The objective of the Foundation is to make the open source software Defold available to the public. For this objective, the Foundation shall keep the software’s source codes available to be used by third parties. Any rights to any results or products in which such source codes have been included, shall always vest in such developing party and may be used for private and commercial purposes. However, the Foundation shall not allow a third party to commercialize the software’s source code, neither as a game engine nor as a development tool, regardless if it has been changed or modified. In addition, the Foundation is not entitled to assign or transfer the rights to the open source software Defold, in addition to what follows from the open licensing which is provided through the software, other than if the Foundation is dissolved, whereby the rights shall pass to the Founder. The Foundation shall manage Defold by, inter alia, update, modify, develop and in other ways support Defold.

The Foundation shall also, inter alia, seek to further the open source society and where possible contribute to the use and development of open source software and similar activities.
