Get Support

Need help? No problem! The developers frequently join our helpful community on the forum and discord. Additionally, at Defold we offer expert guidance, resources, and assistance to help ensure the long-term success of your game.

If you are a game developer...

We are committed to helping developers and studios of all sizes create successful games. We are here to help you throughout your journey. If you are a game developer we offer the following services:

  • Optimization of your game's performance and stability
  • Creating new editor and engine features, as well as new tools
  • Assistance with technical issues or questions that may arise during the development process
  • Porting the game to other platforms, or updating it on existing platforms

With our support, you can focus on creating an engaging and immersive gaming experience while we take care of the technical details, ensuring that your game is lean, performant and stable.

Please contact us at business@defold.se for more information on how we can help you with your game development.

If you are a vendor or a platform...

We understand the importance of seamless integration with the platforms and services that are used for game development. If you are a vendor or a platform we can help you with:

  • Professional guidance on integrating with your platform or product
  • Creating engine and editor integrations with your service
  • Maintaining and keeping the integrations up to date
  • Providing expert assistance in designing and implementing middleware for game engines

With our support, you can have the peace of mind that your product is seamlessly integrated into Defold, allowing you to focus on what you do best: creating a great gaming service!

Please contact us at business@defold.se for more information on how we can help you with your platform and vendor integration.

If you are a game publisher or distributor...

We understand how important it is for publishers to be able to effortlessly integrate popular analytics, app economy and game services in their games. If you are a game publisher we offer the following:

  • Extensive list of battle tested service integrations ready for use in Defold games
  • Additional integrations created based on your needs
  • Compliance with new platform requirements, such as from iOS and Android OS updates
  • Assistance with technical issues or questions that may arise during or after the publishing processs

With our support, you can rest assured that the services you rely on are well integrated and kept up-to-date in your games, letting you focus fully on launching and operating the games in your portfolio.

Please contact us at business@defold.se for more information on how we can help you publish great games made with Defold.
